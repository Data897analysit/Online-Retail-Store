## 📌 Project Overview

An online retail store hired me as a consultant to review their data and provide insights valuable to the **CEO and CMO**. The business has been performing well and management wants to analyze the major contributing factors to revenue for **strategic planning next year.**

The dashboard helps leadership:
- 📊 View metrics from **operations & marketing** perspective
- 🌍 Identify **high-performing regions** for business expansion
- 👥 Understand **top customer** performance metrics
- 📅 Analyze **seasonal revenue trends** for better planning

---

## 🎯 Background & Goals

### Background

| 👔 CEO | Understand overall business performance & revenue trends |
| 📢 CMO | Identify top customers & high-performing countries for targeting |

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📅 Revenue Trend Analysis
| Month | Revenue | Status |
|-------|---------|--------|
| November | $1.51M | 🥇 Highest |
| October | High | 🥈 Peak Season |
| February | Lowest | ⚠️ Needs Attention |

> 💡 **Insight:** November revenue is **188% higher** than February — seasonal promotions should focus on **October-November period**

---

### 🌍 Country-wise Performance
- 🥇 **Netherlands** — 201K units sold (Top performer)
- Strong markets identified across **10+ countries**
- Country-wise **revenue & quantity** both analyzed
- Clear opportunities for **business expansion** in top regions

> 💡 **Insight:** Focus expansion efforts on Netherlands and other top-performing countries

---

### 👥 Top 10 Customer Analysis
- **Top 10 high-value customers** identified
- Customer segmentation helps target **high-revenue buyers**
- Retention strategies can be built around these customers
- **Demographic insights** used for targeted marketing campaigns

> 💡 **Insight:** Building loyalty programs for top customers can significantly boost repeat revenue

---

### 📦 Product & Demand Analysis
- Identified **high-demand products** across regions
- Product performance varies significantly by **country & season**
- Stock optimization possible based on **demand forecasting**

## 🚀 Recommendations

### For CEO
- ✅ Expand business operations in **Netherlands & top countries**
- ✅ Focus Q4 investments on **October-November** peak season
- ✅ Investigate **low-performing months** (Jan-Feb) for improvement

### For CMO
- ✅ Build **loyalty programs** for Top 10 high-value customers
- ✅ Run targeted campaigns in **high-performing regions**
- ✅ Introduce **discount strategies** in slow months to boost revenue
- ✅ Use **country-wise insights** for localized marketing campaigns
